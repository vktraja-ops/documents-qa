INSURANCE DOCUMENT
POLICY NOTE AND COVER NOTE

Insurance Document: can be defined as a document that serve as proof of business insurance.

It is insurance policy that details it helps to protect against third party risk.

Insurance Policy Note: Is an essential contract between you and your insurance company .it lays out what’s covered, what isn’t and other details of your agreement.
Cover Notes: Can be defined as a temporary document issued by an insurance company that provides proof of insurance coverage until a final insurance policy can be issued.
Insurance Certificate: Insurance certificate can be defined as a document that provides the specifications of insurance coverage. It serves as a proof that the insurance is legit.
Insurance Claim: serve to indemnify a policy holder against financial loss. The most insurance claims Involve costs for medical goods and services, physical Damage, loss of life.


2nd week Insurance Scheme
Pension Scheme

Pension: Is a Sum of money paid regularly to a person who no longer work because of age or length of year of service.
The nation pension Scheme is a federal government Policy for the employee of both private and public sectors in which the employee and employer will contribute a satisfactory percentage of the employees monthly Income.

National Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS): Is a Contributory health Insurance Scheme providing medical attention to the contributors which Include an Individual, an employee, the employer and the Government.
 Benefits of (NHIS) National health Insurance Scheme
1) It makes health care delivery Services more efficient
2) It gives every citizen an opportunity to benefit from pool of contribution.
3) It provides equal opportunity to opportunity to medical treatment in health care institution
4) It makes health care service affordable to the people
5) It makes health car service affordable to the people
6) It brings about consideration improvement on the health status of the people.


Week 3
Types of INSURANCE Policy
Accident Insurance
1. Accident Insurance: is a type of insurance where the policy holder is paid directly in the event of an accident resulting in injury of the insured.
2. GUARANTEE INSURANCE: This type of insurance is used as security for the performance of a piece of work which has been agreed upon in a contract.
3. Fidelity coverage can be defined as fidelity bond is a type of insurance that will protect a business owner against theft of money property forgery or fraud by an employee
4. Agricultural insurance: is the protection of Nigerian farmers against the effects of national hazards through the operation of a mechanism that ensues an indemnity by way of compensation sufficient to keep the farmers in business after suffering loss.
5. Export credit insurance: is the type of insurance help exporters grow their international sales and empower them to better manage their business.
